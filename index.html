About The Tournament

Where The World Cup Meets The Command Line

CyberCup 2026 is the flagship IT fest of the Department of Computer Applications — built on the structure, ceremony, and competitive spirit of football's biggest stage, and aimed squarely at technology.

Every fixture on the calendar is a different test of skill: tactical decision-making, competitive programming, creative storytelling, and virtual football — each run with the discipline of a real tournament: fixed brackets, fixed rules, and one winner per event.

Whether you step in as a solo manager or line up as a team, CyberCup gives every participant a real shot at the trophy that matches their game.

FT

Format

Four standalone competitions, each with its own bracket, rules, and trophy — enter one or enter all four.

EL

Eligibility

Open to all currently enrolled students. Team composition rules are fixed per event — check before you register.

AW

Awards

Winners and runners-up are recognized event-wise, with the overall spirit of CyberCup celebrating every fixture equally.

EN

Entry

Registration is online, free of paperwork, and takes under three minutes per event.
